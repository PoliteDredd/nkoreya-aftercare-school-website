
# Nkoreya After-care Tutoring Services Website #
The Nkoreya After-care Tutoring Services Website is a modern, responsive web application designed to showcase the school's vision, academics, and community life. Built to provide parents, students, and staff with easy access to essential information, the site highlights the school's nurturing environment, creative learning programs, and commitment to excellence since 1995.

## ✨Features ##
Home Page: Inspiring introduction with the school's mission and highlights.

About Us: Background, values, and history of Nkoreya After-care Tutoring Services.

Academics: Curriculum details and focus on holistic education.

Admissions: Online enrollment information and application process.

Gallery: Visual showcase of classrooms, playgrounds, arts, and student life.

Contact Page: Location, contact details, and office hours.

Parent Portal & Admin Login: Secure access for parents and staff.

Events & News: Updates on concerts, exhibitions, STEM initiatives, and community projects.

## 🏫What Makes It Special ##
Nurturing Environment: Warm, caring atmosphere where every child feels valued.

Modern Facilities: Safe playgrounds, creative arts spaces, and a new STEM lab.

Personalized Learning: Small class sizes (15:1 student-teacher ratio).

Community Engagement: Initiatives like the community garden project and district science fair participation.
